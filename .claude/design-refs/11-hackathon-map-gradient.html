Gradient Intensity Map

Cities glow brighter with more hackathons & wins. Paris burns hottest.

A. Single Color Heat

One color (pink), intensity = event count. Paris blazes white-hot at the center. Simple, elegant, data-driven. The map tells the story: "this person lives and breathes Paris hackathons."

B. Prismatic Gradient

Two dimensions: SIZE = event count, COLOR = win level. Grey → teal → pink → full prismatic. Paris reaches prismatic mode with its 12 events and multiple wins. Fits your iridescent brand perfectly.

Interaction on hover

Both options would show a tooltip on hover with: hackathon name, project name, result, and domain. For Paris (12 events), hovering cycles through or shows a stacked list.
