Hackathon Map Style

How should the dotted-map render your 24 hackathon locations?

A. Minimal Glow

Subtle dot grid for continents. Prismatic color-coded pins with soft radial glow. Each color = a domain (AI, Quantum, Music, etc). Clean and quiet.

B. Connected Arcs

Dotted continents + curved arc paths connecting hackathon locations chronologically. Shows the "journey" between events. Pins have animated rings. More dynamic, storytelling-oriented.

C. Pulse + Timeline Scrubber

Map + GSAP timeline scrubber at bottom. Drag the playhead to scrub through time — pins appear/pulse as you pass their date. Wins glow green, participations glow pink. Interactive and playful.

These can be combined — e.g. arcs + timeline scrubber. Pick the core vibe and we'll refine.
